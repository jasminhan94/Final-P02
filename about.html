WHO WE ARE

The creative collective studio 'Studio Create' is a new art lab launched by the artist group that formed with celebrities, creative art director, photographer, videographer, screenwriter, music producer, performer, fashion and graphic designer. We focuses on imaginative stories with SPACE as its main concept, and all the artworks and fashion collections will be presented concurrently as various episodes.

The most potential customers are both male and female, age between early-twenties to early forties, who are interested in artistic products and projects and always want something distinct from current trends.

Studio Create always try to produce the distinct projects and products to the audience and customers. All of them are designed collaboratively by famous designers and directors.
