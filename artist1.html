HONG SICK EOM

Hong Sick Eom is a South Korean actor, also known as the director of Studio Create. For his work in film and television, he won best actor at the Blue Dragon Film Awards and Baeksang Arts Awards respectively.Considered one of the most outspoken and politically minded Korean actors of his generation, his unconventional image was strengthened by his willingness to make his social, artistic and philosophical interests public.
