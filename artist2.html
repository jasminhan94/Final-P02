Jerry Jordan

Jerry Jordan, better known by his stage name JJ, is a rapper, singer-songwriter, record producer, and fashion icon. Since becoming an artist, JJ has maintained tight control over his music, stating that "each feeling [in creating the music] is different", using his emotions to direct the lyrical content and the composition of his songs. Additionally, BillBoard described his productions as large-scale, dramatic and infused with his personality.
