KEY.B

Kim Ki-bum better known by his stage name Key.B, is a singer, actor, fashion designer and television presenter. As a singer, he had done many collaborations with various artists, and vastly contributed in songwriting. In Studio Create, Key.B progressed to become a fashion designer and took part in numerous projects. For instance, he collaborated with Jerry Jordan to design his concert outfits, and worked with model Eom and to design fashion apparels. Due to the successes of his works, Key was later appointed as fashion director of the group.
