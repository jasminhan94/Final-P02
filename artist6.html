MARSHA

Marsha is an artist whose photographs, collages, drawings, sculptures and films are concerned with the nature of perception and the contemporary conditions for images. As an photographer, she had done many collaborations with various artists, and vastly contributed in collages. In Studio Create, Marsha progressed to become a fashion designer and took part in numerous projects. For instance, she will be collaborating with Geo.Condo to design her exhibition pieces. Due to the successes of her works, she was later appointed as space design director of the group.
