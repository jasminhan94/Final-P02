MADEMOISELLE CHOUETTE

<<< go back to events page

DETAILED DESCRIPTION

Marsha is an artist whose photographs, collages, drawings, sculptures and films are concerned with the nature of perception and the contemporary conditions for images. This exhibition surveys works produced by Marsha as a space deginer and collaborated with the other artists of Studio Create over the last year and is the first major exhibition of her work in Canada.
