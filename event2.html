DETAILED DESCRIPTION

This is one of the most important exhibition of Geo.Condo’s work. Together with his peers of Studio Create, this exhibition will present thirty-eight paintings spanning the course of Condo’s long career from the unparalleled collection. These works attest to Condo’s dedicated experimentation toward a novel approach to painting, which sought to capture the fleeting appearances and colours conjured by variable light. The exhibition will culminate in a major series of paintings executed in Vancouver. This series reveals his astute and sustained visual exploration of plants and water. His distinctive renderings of weeping willows, waterlilies and the Japanese bridge in the city are among the most iconic imagery in Western painting today. The Vancouver showing will be the only presentation of the exhibition in North America.
